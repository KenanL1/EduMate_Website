Our Humble Beginnings

EduMate started off during the FUN Prepr COVID19 challenge in response to the closure of school. As a result of this education for some students might pose a problem. Some students may require more help then others. Others see the lack of communicate with their peers or have trouble solving a problem. Edumate was made so that students can access school materials online, chat with others their grade, ask for help and test their knowledge on school material.
